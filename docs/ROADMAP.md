# Product roadmap

## Phase 1: MVP foundation

- Authentication, role-based permissions, and secure sessions
- Customer registration and duplicate checks
- Loan application, submission, approval, decline, and disbursement
- Repayment recording and automatic loan closure
- Dashboard and audit log
- Persistent local data storage and Docker deployment

## Phase 2: Operational controls

- Multi-level approval rules and maker-checker controls
- Configurable loan products and repayment schedules
- Guarantors, collateral, documents, and KYC review
- Cashier till management and transaction reversals
- Arrears ageing, recovery tasks, statements, and receipts

## Phase 3: Financial platform

- PostgreSQL-backed immutable ledger
- Double-entry accounting and reconciliation
- Mobile money, bank, SMS, email, and USSD integrations
- Regulatory reports, complaint handling, and customer portal
- Multi-tenant institution isolation and cloud deployment
